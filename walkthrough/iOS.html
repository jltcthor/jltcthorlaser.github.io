1. Select the signature at the bottom of the email.

Gently hold your a finger against the word "Best" and then tap "Select All". Once it has selected everything tap in empty space to bring up the black bar and tap "Copy".

2. Open the Settings app, scroll down and select Mail, then scroll down and select Signatures.

3. Find the space for your THOR account. Click in the empty area and type in your name (and a sign off if you want one). Then use leave two lines and tap on the new line and paste in the signature.

4. Shake your phone (a bit like you're shaking unopened fresh juice). Select "Undo Change Attributes".

5. Now delete where it says "Best Regards, Your Name."

Enjoy.
