1. Select the signature at the bottom of the email.

Copy the selected signature using your preferred method (⌘-C, right-click > copy, etc).

note: the “best regards” is optional — but if you choose to have a standard sign-off at the end of an email I recommend that you copy the one included and change it.

2. Open preferences (⌘-, or Mail > Preferences)

3. Open Signatures and select your THOR account and click the + Symbol. Name the signature.

important: uncheck the option under the signature that says “Always match my default message font”.

4. Delete whatever pre-made signature there is. Paste the new signature in using your preferred method

(⌘-V, right-click > paste, etc) (do not worry if the THOR image does not appear and it’s just an empty box)

5. Change where it says “Your Name” to say your name. If you want to, change “Best Regards”, however you should select it separately.

7. Change the “Choose Signature” option to the new signature.

You may need to close the preferences window and re-open it before the signature appears. If you have to do this, make sure you select your THOR account in the left-most pane first.

Close the preferences window.
